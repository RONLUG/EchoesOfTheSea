Game concept

A 2D puzzle adventure game that follows our main character 'Echo' Echo is a siren that can lure and control the sea creatures with her singing voice, which can help her go through her journey of purifying the sea. Such as, solving puzzle, defeating monsters

Story

One day, the sea is polluted with a mysterious matter. A dark fog that spreads through out the entire (ocean) and effect all living things. Echo is on her mission to find the source of this mysterious fog that destroys the sea and restored it back to its natural way
