Game concept

A 2D puzzle adventure game that follows our main character 'Echo' Echo is a siren that can lure and control the sea creatures with her singing voice, which can help her go through her journey of purifying the sea. Such as, solving puzzle, defeating monsters

Story

One day, the sea is polluted with a mysterious matter. A dark fog that spreads through out the entire (ocean) and effect all living things. Echo is on her mission to find the source of this mysterious fog that destroys the sea and restored it back to its natural way

Ventured to the depth

Echo

Echo is a siren that can lure and control the sea creatures with her singing voice. Echo is designed to represent the light in this dark sea. She has the appearance of the creature in the same name in western mythology: half human, half fish. Echo's unique point is her hair that resemble a fish's fin and tw fins on both side of her tail.

Lure

Lure is a skill that is with Echo since the beginning of the game. When Echo is using Lure, she will create circle sound wave that will lure the sea creatures around her into the circle. These sea creatures can help her go through obstacles.
